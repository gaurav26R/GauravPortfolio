About Me

Hi, I'm Gaurav Patil — A passionate and results-driven Full Stack Developer based in Pune, originally from Amalner, Maharashtra. I specialize in Java Full Stack Development and have hands-on experience working with technologies like Java, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, and Git.

My academic journey culminated in a capstone project titled "Privacy-Preserving Media Sharing with Scalable Access Control and Deduplication in Cloud Computing". This project tackled real-world challenges in cloud-based media sharing by ensuring robust privacy, scalable access management, and efficient data deduplication.

I thrive on solving complex problems with clean and scalable solutions, whether it's building dynamic front-end interfaces or architecting reliable back-end systems. I’m always eager to learn, grow, and contribute to impactful, innovative projects that create real value.

Beyond coding, I enjoy exploring hill stations, learning new technologies, and working on projects that challenge me to think differently. I believe in the power of consistent learning and am always looking for new ways to expand my skills and grow as a developer.
